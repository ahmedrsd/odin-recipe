BQ salmon Bowls

Description

A teriyaki salmon fillet is cooked in the
air fryer (or grill/oven), set on top of a bed of rice, and
topped with avocado, cucumber, mango, edamame, green onions, and cilantro

Ingredients

  • 1 medium ripe mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 3/4 chopped red bell pepper
  • canned tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon lime zest
  • spices and seasonings
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 teaspoons honey

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees C
    (230 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil.
    Coat foil with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. For mango salsa, combine mango, red pepper, green onion, lime zest and
    j uice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, habanero, basil, honey, and
    1/4 teaspoon salt in a bowl.
  3. For BBQ spice mixture, combine brown sugar, chili powder, smoked paprika, onion powder, cumin, and
    remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt in a small bowl.
  4. Place salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
    Brush with remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil.
    Sprinkle generously with BBQ spice mixture.
  5. Bake until fish flakes easily when tested with a
    fork, 4 to 6 minutes per 1/2-inch thickness of fish.
  6. Arrange rice and beans in four shallow serving bowls. Top with salmon and salsa.
    Arrange avocado and red onion in the bowls. Serve and enjoy.
